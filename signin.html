HCTG Turn Your Radio On Treasure Hunt!


Maths round! (of course there's a maths round!)

Start with the year of the most recent Aladdin took place.
Multiply by the number in Ned's surname.
Minus the number of cousins in Me And My Ron.
Divide by the number of letters in Bingo's mantra.
Add the number of letters in the name of Bobby's band.
Add the number of letters in the response to Fu Manchu's name.
Last, perform the operation that describes the kind of vegetable that is Ron's posh pseudonym...



Now go to B-Solo.github.io/XXXXXXXXXX.html where you replace the Xs with the number you ended up with! Good luck!

(This was not intended to be a lesson on the order of operations, just do each instruction in order!)
