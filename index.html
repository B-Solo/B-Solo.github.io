HCTG Turn Your Radio On Treasure Hunt!

Welcome to the HCTG Turn Your Radio On Treasure Hunt!

Solving the clues on this website will lead you to locations around the theatre. If you're in the right place, you'll find a QR code to a website with the next clue!

The clues will be changed to get easier as the days go on to help people who get stuck!

The first three countries that solve the final puzzle at the end win the medals - so work together with your teammates, share the websites/clues, and discuss with each other!
